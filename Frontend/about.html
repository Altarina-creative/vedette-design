About Vedette Designs

Vedette Designs is a creative branding & packaging studio delivering end-to-end visual solutions for brands looking to stand out in competitive markets.

Overview

Who We Are

Vedette Designs specializes in branding, packaging design, pre-media services, and creative visual solutions. We work closely with clients to ensure brand consistency, premium quality output, and market-ready designs across all platforms.

Our expertise covers packaging design, brand identity, print-ready artworks, mockups, and digital creatives. We believe in combining creativity with strong technical knowledge to deliver results that add real business value.

Founder Profile

Professional Background

Sunil Singh is the founder and driving force behind Vedette Designs. He has over 14 years of professional experience in the packaging, prepress, and printing industry.

He has worked with leading organizations across India and international markets, handling complex packaging projects, color management, artwork adaptation, and production-ready workflows.

His expertise includes mono cartons, corrugated boxes, labels, flexible packaging, sleeves, and large-scale print operations. He has successfully handled client requirements for global brands while maintaining strict quality and deadline standards.
